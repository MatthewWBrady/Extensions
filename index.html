Common questions.

What platforms does this work on?

The Underdog Overlay works exclusively on Underdog Fantasy (underdogfantasy.com). It does not work on other fantasy platforms.

How does my exposure data get into the extension?

Navigate to your exposure page on Underdog and click the Scrape button in the extension popup. Your ownership data is pulled directly from the page and stored locally in your browser.

Do I need to re-scrape every draft?

No — your data persists until you clear it. Just re-scrape whenever you want to update your ownership numbers after completing more drafts.
